Recipe for air fried potsticker

Ingredients

  • 1/2 ground pork
  • 1(4 ounce) can of water chestnusts, drained and chopped
  • 1(4 ounce) can shiitake mushrooms, drained and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablspoons of ssiracha sauce
  • 1(12 ounce) package round dumpling wrappers

Directions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F(200 degrees C).
  2. combine all ingredients above in a large skillet over medium to high heat. Cook until pork is done. Afterwards remove from heat and let sit until cool enough to handle.
  3. lay out 8 dumpling wrappers on cleand surface. Place a heaping teaspoonful of pork mixture in the middle of each wrapper. Pull both sides up like a taco and pinch the tops until sealed.
  4. Cook in batches in the pre-heated air fryer for 3 minutes. Use tongs to flip the potstickers and cook 3 minutes more. Transfer to a paper-towel lined plate. Repeat with remaining dumpling wrappers and filling.
