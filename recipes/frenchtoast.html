Recipe for french toast

Here you will learn about what is needed to make delicous french toast.

Ingredients

  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. Beat egg, milk salt, desired spices and vanilla together.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  4. Place in pan, cook on both sides until golden.
  5. Serve hot.
