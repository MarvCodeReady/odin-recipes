Recepie for waffles

Here you will learn about what is needed to make delicious Waffles.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 3/4 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 4 teaspoons of backing powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat waffle iron.
  2. beat egss in a large bowl with hand beater.
  3. Add and beat in flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt, and vanilla untill smooth.
  4. spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray.
  5. Cook unitl golden brown.
  6. serve until hot.
